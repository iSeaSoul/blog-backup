败给了栈

18th of January 2015

tips cplusplus

早上起来做HackCup，败给了递归爆栈，忘了怎么加栈深度。做点记录，留着以后查。

C++： 

#pragma comment(linker, "/STACK:1024000000,1024000000")

G++： 

int size = 256 << 20; // 256MB
char *p = (char*)malloc(size) + size;
__asm__("movl %0, %%esp\n" :: "r"(p));
