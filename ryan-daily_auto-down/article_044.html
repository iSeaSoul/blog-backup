Summary & Prospect

2th of December 2014

summary daily-note

After almost three weeks being there, I have regained the rhythm of working, never too bad, and of course not so good.

The most pleasant fact is getting plenty of free time beyond working unlike the previous days, while unfortunately, most time was wasted, because of the bad habits I had developed from the free days juggling with myself.

If the pace was keeping in the level, I don't think the saved time was meaningful, why you change the job?

To move forward, to chase for tranquilness.

So I propose some proposals:

  1. Recording everyday
  2. Running everyday 9 P.M.
  3. A poem everyday
  4. A long summary every weekend
  5. A new book every week

Let's dance!
