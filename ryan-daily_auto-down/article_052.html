A. Dreamoon and Stairs

Climb up a stair of n steps with 1 or 2 steps one move. What's the minimal moves that is a multiple of interger m.

The minimal number of moves is $\frac {n + 1} {2}$, make it a multiple of m and check if it's less than n.

B. Dreamoon and WiFi

Given two operation strings, '+' indicates moving forward, '-' indicates moving back, and '?' indicates equal probability to choose either. Calculate the probablity that they both result in a same position.

Simple DP. DP[i][j] indicates the probability that after i moves ends in position j. For a '?' character, tranform the state to either direction.

C. Dreamoon and Sums

$\sum(X | mod(X, b) != 0 \bigwedge div(X, b) = k*mod(X, b) | k \subset [1, a])$ .

Simple Math. Assume the number X is $ k1*b + k2 $. If k2 = 1, so $ k \subset [1, a] $, the sum is $\frac{(1 + a) * a}{2} * b + a$. And as $ k2 \subset [1, b - 1]$, the final ans is $(\frac{(1 + a) * a}{2} * b + a) * \frac {b * (b - 1)} {2}$.

D. Dreamoon and Sets

Make up n sets with four integers of rank k using integers from 1 to m such that no integer is used in two different sets, with the minimum m.

The key is ${ 6 k+1, 6 k+2, 6 k+3, 6 k+5 }$ is always a valid set.

E. Dreamoon and Strings

Given two strings S and T, if delete 0 - len(s) characters for string S, calculate the maximal number of non-overlapping substrings equal to T in reduced S.

First calculate a minlen[i] assisted array which indicates the minimal length to match T starting from index i. It can be solved greedily.

Then do a dp[i][j] indicates the maximum match that ends at index i and delete j characters. So for a state dp[i][j] , it can contribute to three state:

  • dp[i + 1][j] means just keep that character
  • dp[i + 1][j + 1] means delete that character
  • dp[i + minlen[i]][j + minlen[i] - lent] means make one matching with minimal removed character, and the value should be increased by 1

Then the dp[lens][0...lens] would be the answer.
