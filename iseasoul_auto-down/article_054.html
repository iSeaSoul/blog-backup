Decisive Decision

8th of March 2012

decision 心情


I know, the life I yearn for, absolutely out of ordinary thought.

So, I ought to pay something that others hadn't paid for compensation, even been misunderstood, even been deserted by the world, I should not be regretful.

For, that's the purity I expected solely, and that's the only way placing myself at ease.

I, never yield.

(version original)

我自知，我所期待的生活，绝非常人所愿。

那我理应为之付出别人所不曾付出的东西，即使不被理解，即使被世界抛弃，我也应无怨无悔。

因为，那才是我期待的纯粹，那才能让我安心。

我，永不会妥协。
